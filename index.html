About Me

I am a third-year physics student. I've been interested in physics since high school, and I'll admit that it is an elusive but challenging science. While in college, I started to understand that the scope was vast, so I picked out a section that was really interesting and that I wanted to explore.

I took part in several campus activities such as Badan Eksekutif Mahasiswa Fakultas Matematika dan Ilmu Pengetahuan Alam (BEM FMIPA), IPB Robotic Club (IRC), and Physics Research Club (PRC). There are lots of activities on campus, so I joined several committees, and that was fun. I am interested in robotics, especially electronics filed.
